Privacy Solutions for Smart Devices

Use of Strong Passwords and Authentication

By utilising strong passwords for all the smart devices connected as well as the associated accounts it will help decrease the likelihood of unauthorised users gaining access which is useful. Furthermore, the use of multi-factor authentication is efficient since it helps provide an extra layer of security and is a strong measure to implement.

Perform Software Updates Regulary

By regulary carrying out the neccesary software updates on your smart devices it will help patch any type of vulnerabilites that were known and will help improve security, so ensure smart devices are always up-to-date in terms of software.

Secure Wi-fi Network

Initally set strong passwords on the Wi-Fi network, create a seperate network simply just for the smart devices which helps them remain seperate from your main devices. this is an efficient method since it prevents the attackers from gaining any further acces to any other devices on the network since the smart devices are isolated.

Limit Data Collection and Sharing

Review and change the settings of privacy on the smart devices to be able to limit the amount of data it collects and shares, as well as deactivate any functions that are not in use as well as review the permission granted towards the smart device.

Physical Security

Ensure the smart devices are safe and securee phyiscally as well as are not accessbile towards unauthorised people such as by placing them in out hidden areas or even lockable cases.

Remain Informed

Always try to keep informed of the latest privacy practices that can be performed to imporve the overall security of the smart devices as well as provide and spread awareness to others about the importance of privacy as well as security in terms of smart devices.
